<p><p>The EV charging RFID card market comprises two primary types: prepaid RFID cards and subscription RFID cards. Prepaid RFID cards allow users to load a specific amount of credit for charging their electric vehicles, offering flexibility and control over expenses. In contrast, subscription RFID cards provide users with a membership-based access model, often including fixed monthly fees for unlimited charging access or discounted rates. Both types enhance the convenience of EV charging, catering to various user preferences and driving habits.</p></p>
