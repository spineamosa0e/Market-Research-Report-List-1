<p><strong>The Fibre Channel Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modular Director Switches(Backbone Switches)</li><li>Semi-modular Switches(Edge Switches)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fibre Channel Switches market consists of two main types: Modular Director Switches and Semi-modular Switches. Modular Director Switches, or Backbone Switches, are high-capacity, scalable solutions used in data centers for core networking, supporting extensive connections and high throughput. Semi-modular Switches, known as Edge Switches, offer flexibility and are typically deployed at the network's edge, connecting servers and storage devices with lower capacity but easier manageability. Together, they provide a comprehensive infrastructure for storage networking.</p></p>
