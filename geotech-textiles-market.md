<p><p>The Geotech Textiles market comprises various types, including woven, non-woven, knitted fabrics, and others. Woven fabrics feature interlaced threads, providing strength and durability for applications like erosion control. Non-woven fabrics are made from fibers bonded together, offering excellent filtration and drainage properties, ideal for soil stabilization. Knitted fabrics consist of interlooped yarns, offering flexibility and comfort, frequently used for geocomposites. The "others" category includes specialty textiles, such as biodegradable options, designed for specific environmental needs.</p></p>
