<p><p>The Fibre Reinforced Polymer (FRP) pole market encompasses various manufacturing techniques, including pultrusion, filament winding, and centrifugal casting. Pultrusion involves continuously drawing fibers through a resin bath, forming strong, lightweight poles ideal for utility and structural applications. Filament winding entails winding resin-impregnated fibers around a rotating mandrel, creating high-strength, customized shapes. Centrifugal casting allows for a uniform distribution of fibers and resin in cylindrical forms, enhancing durability and resistance. Each method offers distinct advantages for diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
