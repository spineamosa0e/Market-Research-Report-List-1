<p><p>The neurointerventional devices market encompasses various types of technologies used in treating neurovascular conditions. Embolic coils are utilized to occlude aneurysms, while neurovascular stents, including carotid and intracranial stents, provide support to narrowed vessels. Intrasaccular devices aid in aneurysm stabilization, and neurothrombectomy devices enable clot removal. Flow diverters redirect blood flow to treat aneurysms. Embolic protection devices prevent debris during procedures, while liquid embolics offer a minimally invasive occlusion option. Balloons assist in vessel dilation, and stent retrievers aid in thrombectomy.</p></p>
