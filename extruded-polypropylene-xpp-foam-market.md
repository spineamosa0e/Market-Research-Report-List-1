<p><p>The Extruded Polypropylene (XPP) Foam market comprises two primary types: low-density XPP foam and high-density XPP foam. Low-density XPP foam is lightweight, flexible, and offers good thermal insulation, making it suitable for applications such as packaging and cushioning. In contrast, high-density XPP foam is more robust, providing enhanced durability and structural support, ideal for uses in automotive interiors, sports equipment, and construction. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of strength, weight, and insulation properties.</p></p>
