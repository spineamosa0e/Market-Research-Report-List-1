<p><p>The Automated Driving Control Unit market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Perception Control Unit** - This unit processes data from sensors to understand the vehicle's environment, including obstacles and road conditions. </p><p>   </p><p>2. **Decision Control Unit** - It analyzes the perception data to make driving decisions, like navigation paths and speed adjustments. </p><p>3. **Actuator Control Unit** - This unit translates decisions into actions by controlling the vehicle's steering, brakes, and acceleration systems, ensuring smooth and safe operation.</p></p>
