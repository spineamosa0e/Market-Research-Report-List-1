<p><p>The industrial thermal continuous furnaces market encompasses various types, including belt furnaces, rotating hearth furnaces, and pusher furnaces. Belt furnaces utilize continuous conveyor systems for efficient heating and processing of materials. Rotating hearth furnaces feature a rotating base that ensures uniform heat distribution for advanced thermal treatments. Pusher furnaces rely on mechanical systems to move materials through designated heating zones. Other furnace types include batch and chamber designs, catering to diverse applications in industries such as metallurgy, ceramics, and glass.</p></p>
