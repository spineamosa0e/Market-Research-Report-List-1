<p><p>Survey tools are categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise survey tools are installed and run locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security but requiring extensive maintenance and IT resources. Conversely, Cloud-Based survey tools are hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, providing scalability, automatic updates, and easier collaboration. Each type caters to different organizational needs, with the choice often depending on budget, resources, and desired flexibility.</p></p>
