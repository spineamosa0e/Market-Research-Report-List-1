<p><strong>The Veterinary Endoscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Endoscopy</li><li>Rigid Endoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary endoscopy market is divided into two main types: flexible endoscopy and rigid endoscopy. Flexible endoscopy utilizes a flexible tube with a light and camera, allowing for minimally invasive examination of internal organs in animals, such as the gastrointestinal tract. Rigid endoscopy, on the other hand, involves a straight, non-flexible tube for visualizing and accessing specific areas, such as joints or the respiratory system. Both methods enhance diagnostic capabilities and improve treatment options in veterinary medicine.</p></p>
