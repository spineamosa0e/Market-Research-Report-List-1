<p><p>The Industrial Solid Lasers market is primarily categorized into two types: pulse type and continuous type. Pulse type lasers emit energy in short bursts, making them ideal for high-precision applications such as engraving, cutting, and welding, where heat management is crucial. In contrast, continuous type lasers produce a steady beam of light, which is suitable for processes requiring sustained energy, such as marking and drilling. Each type offers distinct advantages tailored to specific industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
