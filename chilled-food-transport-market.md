<p><p>Chilled food transport is divided into two primary market types: single temperature and multi-temperature. The single temperature market involves transporting perishable goods at a consistent temperature, ideal for products with similar cooling requirements. In contrast, the multi-temperature market allows for the simultaneous transport of various food items at different temperatures, accommodating diverse perishability needs. This flexibility is crucial for maintaining food safety and quality while maximizing delivery efficiency in the supply chain.</p></p>
