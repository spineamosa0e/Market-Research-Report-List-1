<p><p>A Computer On Module (COM) is a compact, integrated computing system designed to streamline the development and manufacturing of embedded devices. COMs typically consist of a CPU, memory, and essential I/O interfaces, all integrated into a single module. They provide a flexible solution for various applications, including industrial automation, medical devices, and consumer electronics, allowing developers to easily upgrade or replace the core processing unit without redesigning the entire device.</p><p>The Computer On Module (COM) market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for embedded systems across diverse industries.
