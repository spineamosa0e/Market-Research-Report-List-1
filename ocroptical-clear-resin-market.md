<p><p>The Optical Clear Resin (OCR) market includes two primary types: silicone-based and acrylic-based resins. Silicone-based OCRs are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to temperature variations, making them suitable for applications in electronics and automotive sectors. In contrast, acrylic-based OCRs offer superior optical clarity and are often favored for applications in displays and lighting. Both types are utilized in various industries for encapsulation and bonding purposes, driven by the increasing demand for high-performance optical materials.</p></p>
