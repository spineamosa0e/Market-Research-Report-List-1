<p><p>Gears are crucial mechanical components used for transmitting motion and torque in various applications. The gear market can be categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Spur Gears**: Straight-toothed gears for parallel shafts.  </p><p>2. **Helical Gears**: Angled teeth for smoother engagement.  </p><p>3. **Bevel Gears**: Connect shafts at right angles.  </p><p>4. **Worm Gears**: Provide high torque with low speed.  </p><p>5. **Gear Racks**: Convert rotational motion to linear motion.  </p><p>6. **Others**: Includes specialized and custom gears for niche applications. Each type serves distinct purposes across industries.</p></p>
