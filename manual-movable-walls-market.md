<p><p>The Manual Movable Walls market can be categorized into two main types: hard material and soft material. Hard material movable walls are typically constructed from durable materials like wood, glass, or metal, providing robust sound insulation and enhanced privacy. They are ideal for permanent or semi-permanent installations in commercial spaces. These are often used in dynamic environments where space needs frequently change.</p></p>
