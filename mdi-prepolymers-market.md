<p><p>The MDI prepolymers market is categorized into two main types: MDI polyether and MDI polyester. MDI polyether prepolymers are primarily used in applications requiring flexibility, such as in flexible polyurethane foams for cushioning and insulation. In contrast, MDI polyester prepolymers offer greater rigidity and durability, making them suitable for coatings, adhesives, and rigid foam applications. Both types are essential components in various industries, including automotive, construction, and furniture, driven by their distinct properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
