<p><ul><li>Short Fiber Type</li><li>Long Fiber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linen yarn market is divided into two main types based on fiber length: short fiber type and long fiber type. Short fiber type, derived from the outer stem of the flax plant, is often used in low-cost textiles and woven fabrics, providing a coarse texture. In contrast, long fiber type, sourced from the inner stem, offers superior strength, durability, and a smoother finish, making it ideal for high-end apparel and luxury fabrics. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
