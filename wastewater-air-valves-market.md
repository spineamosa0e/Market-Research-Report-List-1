<p><strong>The Wastewater Air Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Hole Wastewater Air Valves</li><li>Two Holes Wastewater Air Valves</li><li>Three Holes Wastewater Air Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wastewater air valves market comprises three main types: single hole, two holes, and three holes wastewater air valves. Single hole valves are designed for basic venting functions, allowing air to escape or enter the pipeline. Two holes valves facilitate both air release and intake, enhancing system efficiency. Three holes valves provide the most versatility, effectively managing air release, intake, and preventing vacuum. Each type serves distinct purposes in wastewater management, ensuring optimal system performance and preventing air-related issues.</p></p>
