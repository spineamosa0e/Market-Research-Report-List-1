<p><ul><li>Mechanical Gyro Technology</li><li>Ring Laser Gyro Technology</li><li>Fiber Optics Gyro Technology</li><li>MEMS Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inertial Navigation Systems (INS) utilize various technologies to determine an object's position and orientation without external references. Mechanical Gyro Technology relies on rotating mass gyroscopes, providing reliable navigation for aircraft and ships. Ring Laser Gyro Technology employs lasers in a closed loop for high precision, commonly used in spacecraft. Fiber Optic Gyro Technology uses light in optical fibers for accurate and stable navigation. MEMS Technology offers miniaturized sensors, enabling cost-effective and compact solutions in consumer electronics and vehicles.</p></p>
