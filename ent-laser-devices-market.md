<p><p>The ENT laser devices market includes three main types: gas lasers, solid-state lasers, and semiconductor-based lasers. Gas lasers, such as CO2 lasers, are commonly used for cutting and vaporizing tissue with precision. Solid-state lasers, often employing crystals or glass, provide high efficiency and powerful output, suitable for various ENT procedures. Semiconductor-based lasers, utilizing diodes, offer compact designs and versatility, making them ideal for applications requiring fine control in delicate surgeries. Together, these technologies enhance surgical outcomes in ENT practices.</p></p>
