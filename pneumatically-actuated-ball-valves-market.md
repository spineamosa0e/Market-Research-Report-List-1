<p><p>The pneumatically actuated ball valves market encompasses various connection types, each suited for specific applications. Threaded connections provide easy installation and maintenance, ideal for smaller diameters. Flanged connections offer robust sealing for larger pipelines, ensuring high-pressure performance. Butt weld connections are used for a seamless flow path, suitable for high-temperature applications, while socket weld connections enable a strong bond with reduced risk of leakage, often utilized in compact spaces. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing valve performance.</p></p>
