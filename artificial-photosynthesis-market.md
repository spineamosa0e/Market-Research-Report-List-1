<p><p>The artificial photosynthesis market features three primary types. Suspended nanpowder photocatalysts use fine particles to enhance light absorption and catalyze chemical reactions, improving efficiency in converting CO2 to fuels. Photovoltaic cell-driven electrolysers harness solar energy to split water into hydrogen and oxygen, creating sustainable fuels. Photoelectrochemical cells (PECs) integrate photovoltaics with electrolysis, directly converting sunlight and water into hydrogen. Each type offers unique advantages for renewable energy production and carbon reduction, driving innovation in clean energy technologies.</p></p>
