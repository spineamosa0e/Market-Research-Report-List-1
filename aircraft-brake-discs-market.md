<p><p>The aircraft brake discs market comprises primarily two types: carbon-ceramic and carbon-carbon materials. Carbon-ceramic brake discs are composed of a composite of carbon and ceramic, offering high durability, reduced weight, and superior thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance aircraft. On the other hand, carbon-carbon brakes consist of carbon fibers reinforced with carbon, providing exceptional heat resistance and strength, particularly suited for military and high-speed jets. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in aviation braking systems.</p></p>
