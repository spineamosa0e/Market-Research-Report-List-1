<p><p>The burn spray market is divided into two main types: aerosol and non-aerosol. Aerosol burn sprays use pressurized gas to deliver a fine mist, providing an even application and quick relief for minor burns. They are convenient and portable. Non-aerosol burn sprays, on the other hand, often utilize pumps or other mechanisms for application, allowing for controlled dosage and often containing fewer chemicals. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs in burn care management.</p></p>
