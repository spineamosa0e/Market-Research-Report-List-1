<p><p>AMOLED screens, or Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode displays, are categorized into two main types: hard screens and soft screens. Hard AMOLED screens are rigid and typically used in smartphones and tablets, providing excellent image quality and durability. In contrast, soft AMOLED screens are flexible and can be bent or curved, ideal for innovative designs in wearables and futuristic devices. Both types leverage the advantages of AMOLED technology, including vibrant colors, deep blacks, and low power consumption, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
