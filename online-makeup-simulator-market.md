<p><p>The Online Makeup Simulator market consists of two primary types: Augmented Reality (AR) Simulators and Image-Based Simulators. AR Simulators use real-time facial recognition and overlay makeup products onto users' live camera feeds, allowing for a dynamic, interactive experience. In contrast, Image-Based Simulators utilize pre-uploaded images to apply makeup virtually, providing a more static visualization. Both types enhance consumer engagement in the beauty industry, allowing users to experiment with products before purchasing, ultimately driving sales and improving customer satisfaction.</p></p>
