<p><p>Non-destructive testers (NDT) are essential tools used to evaluate the integrity and properties of materials without causing damage. The market comprises various types, including Magnetic Particle Testers, which detect surface and near-surface flaws using magnetic fields; Ultrasonic Testers, which utilize high-frequency sound waves to identify internal defects; and Liquid Penetrant Testers, which reveal surface cracks through dye penetration. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses methods like eddy current testing and radiography, broadening the scope of NDT applications across industries.</p></p>
