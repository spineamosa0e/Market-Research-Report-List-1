<p><p>The Radio Frequency (RF) Tester market encompasses various types of instruments used to analyze and generate radio frequency signals. Network analyzers assess device performance, evaluating parameters like gain and return loss. Spectrum analyzers measure signal amplitudes across frequencies, identifying interference and spectral purity. Oscilloscopes visualize signals in real-time, displaying waveform shapes and timing characteristics. Signal generators produce specific RF signals for testing and calibration purposes, enabling the development and maintenance of communication systems and electronic devices.</p></p>
