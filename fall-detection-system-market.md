<p><p>The fall detection system market comprises three main types: wearable systems, in-home landline systems, and in-home cellular systems. In-home landline systems use traditional phone lines to connect to alert centers when a fall occurs, providing immediate assistance. In-home cellular systems operate through mobile networks, offering greater flexibility and independence, ensuring users can receive help regardless of landline availability.</p></p>
