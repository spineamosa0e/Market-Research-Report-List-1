<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Traffic Simulation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microscopic Simulation</li><li>Mesoscopic Simulation</li><li>Macroscopic Simulation</li><li>Traffic Planning Simulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traffic simulation systems can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Microscopic Simulation** focuses on individual vehicle movements and interactions, providing detailed analysis of traffic flow at a granular level.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Mesoscopic Simulation** balances detail and computational efficiency, modeling groups of vehicles rather than individual ones, capturing overall traffic dynamics while simplifying certain interactions.</p><p>3. **Macroscopic Simulation** analyzes traffic flow on a larger scale, using aggregated data to represent behavior across road networks.</p><p>Additionally, **Traffic Planning Simulation** specifically aids in the design and evaluation of transportation systems, supporting strategic decision-making for future infrastructure development.</p></p>
