<p><p>The car lamp market features three main types: halogen, HID, and LED lamps. Halogen lamps are traditional, offering good brightness at an affordable cost, but with shorter lifespans. High-Intensity Discharge (HID) lamps provide brighter light and greater efficiency but can be more expensive and complex to install. LED lamps are becoming increasingly popular due to their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and versatility, offering a modern alternative with customizable designs, although they often come at a higher initial cost.</p></p>
