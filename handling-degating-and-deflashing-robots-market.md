<p><p>Handling, degating, and deflashing robots are specialized automation solutions used primarily in manufacturing processes involving the removal of excess material from molded parts and the efficient handling of these components. Handling robots manage the movement of products within production lines, while degating robots focus on removing gates and runners from molded products. Deflashing robots are designed to eliminate flash—the unwanted excess material—often found around edges of cast or molded items.</p><p>The Handling, Degating, and Deflashing Robots Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for automation in manufacturing to enhance efficiency and reduce labor costs. Manufacturers are increasingly adopting these robots to streamline workflows, improve product quality, and minimize waste.
