<p><p>Wall cladding artificial grass is available in three primary types: polyethylene, polypropylene, and nylon. Polyethylene offers a realistic appearance and softness, making it ideal for residential applications. Polypropylene is more affordable and lightweight, suitable for temporary installations or low-traffic areas. Nylon is the most durable and resilient, providing excellent resistance to wear and UV rays, making it perfect for high-traffic commercial spaces. Each type caters to different needs, balancing aesthetics, durability, and cost-effectiveness in various environments.</p></p>
