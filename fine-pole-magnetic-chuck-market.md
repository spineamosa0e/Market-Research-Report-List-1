<p><ul><li>Electro-Permanent Chuck</li><li>Permanent Magnet Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fine Pole Magnetic Chuck market includes two main types: Electro-Permanent Chucks and Permanent Magnet Chucks. Electro-Permanent Chucks utilize electrical energy to activate and deactivate magnetism, providing strong clamping and easy handling, making them ideal for high-precision machining. Permanent Magnet Chucks, on the other hand, use permanent magnets to generate a constant magnetic field, offering a reliable and energy-efficient solution for holding ferromagnetic workpieces. Both types enhance productivity and precision in machining applications.</p></p>
