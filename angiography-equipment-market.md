<p><strong>The Angiography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Angiography</li><li>CT Angiography</li><li>MR Angiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiography equipment market includes various imaging modalities to visualize blood vessels. X-ray angiography utilizes fluoroscopy to obtain live images, typically offering high temporal resolution for dynamic studies. CT angiography uses computed tomography to create detailed cross-sectional images of vessels, often providing excellent spatial resolution and speed. MR angiography employs magnetic resonance imaging to assess blood flow and vessel anomalies non-invasively, relying on magnetic fields and radio waves, thus avoiding ionizing radiation. Each type serves specific diagnostic needs in vascular imaging.</p></p>
