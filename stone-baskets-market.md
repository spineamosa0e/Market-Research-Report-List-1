<p><p>The stone baskets market includes two primary types: nitinol and stainless steel stone baskets. Nitinol stone baskets are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to deformation, making them ideal for navigating narrow urethras and performing delicate procedures. In contrast, stainless steel stone baskets offer strength and cost-effectiveness but may lack the same flexibility as nitinol. Both types are essential in urology to retrieve kidney stones, with varying preferences based on specific procedural requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
