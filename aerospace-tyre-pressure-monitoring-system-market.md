<p><p>The Aerospace Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market encompasses two main types: wireless and wired systems. Wireless TPMS utilize sensors that communicate data via radio frequencies, enabling real-time monitoring without the need for extensive wiring, thus simplifying installation and maintenance. In contrast, wired TPMS involve direct connections between sensors and the aircraft's monitoring systems, offering reliable data transmission but requiring more complex installations. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety and performance in aviation by maintaining optimal tyre pressure.</p></p>
